Media Privacy and Local Processing

Local Processing

Eye Color Cam is designed to protect your privacy completely. All core functionality operates locally on your device:

  • AR Eye Color Effects: All augmented reality processing and eye color effects are rendered locally on your device
  • Photo & Video Processing: All photo and video editing with eye color effects occurs entirely on your device
