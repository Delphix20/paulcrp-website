Document Privacy and Local Processing

Local Processing

ProScan PDF is designed to protect your document privacy. All core functionality operates locally on your device:

  • Document Scanning: All camera-based document scanning is processed locally
  • OCR (Optical Character Recognition): Text extraction from documents occurs entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework
  • PDF Creation and Editing: PDF generation, organization, signing, and password protection are performed locally
  • Document Storage: All scanned documents and created PDFs are stored locally on your device

We do not upload, access, or store your documents on our servers. Your scanned documents, OCR text, and PDF files remain private and under your control.

Camera and Photo Library Access

The App requires access to your device camera and photo library to function:

  • Camera Access: Used for scanning documents and capturing images
  • Photo Library Access: Used for importing existing photos to convert to PDFs

This access is used solely for the stated functionality. We do not access other photos or use your camera for any purpose other than document scanning.
