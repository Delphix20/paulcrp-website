If you have any health condition, breathing difficulty, cardiovascular issue, mental health concern, dizziness, pregnancy-related concern, or other relevant medical condition, consult a qualified professional before relying on guidance inside the app.

What Alma Collects and Stores

Data you enter or create

Alma may store information you provide or generate inside the app, including:

  • Your first name, if you choose to enter one
  • Sleep logs you manually enter
  • Hydration logs and selected bottle or goal settings
  • Mood check-ins
  • Breathing session completions
  • Balance score history and related daily wellness records
  • App settings such as reminders, preferences, and selected formats

Health data from Apple Health

If you grant permission, Alma may read selected categories from Apple Health in order to support the app experience. This may include sleep data and step count data.

  • Health access is optional and controlled by you through Apple's permission system
  • Alma uses Apple Health data to help calculate wellness progress and improve balance-related insights
  • Health permissions can be changed or revoked by you at any time in the Health app or iOS settings

Usage and technical data

We may collect limited technical and usage information such as:

  • App version and build information
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics

How Alma Uses Your Data

Alma may use stored or permitted data to:

  • Calculate and display your balance score
  • Show daily and historical wellness information
  • Display hydration, sleep, mood, movement, and breathing progress
  • Generate insights, trends, and recommendations
  • Restore your app experience across your devices if you use iCloud-supported sync
  • Operate subscriptions, restore purchases, and enforce access
  • Maintain app reliability, stability, and support diagnostics

Storage, Sync, and iCloud

Alma stores core app data locally on your device. If iCloud-related features are enabled, Alma may also use Apple services such as CloudKit or iCloud key-value storage to sync selected app data and settings across devices associated with your Apple account.

  • App history and daily wellness records may be synced through Apple's private iCloud services
  • Settings and small preferences may also sync through Apple's iCloud systems
  • Cross-device sync is generally tied to your own Apple account and compatible devices

We do not operate a custom backend for Alma user wellness history at this time unless explicitly stated otherwise inside the app.

Health, Wellness, and Safety Disclaimers

Balance Score

The balance score is a simplified wellness signal based on available app data, logged activity, selected goals, and available Health information. It is informational only and should not be treated as a diagnosis, risk score, or medical evaluation.

Hydration

Hydration guidance in Alma is general and informational. It does not account for medical conditions, medications, climate, exercise load, pregnancy, age, kidney issues, heart conditions, or individualized fluid restrictions. Always follow your doctor's advice or other qualified medical guidance where applicable.

Breathing Exercises

Breathing exercises in Alma are intended for general wellness, calm, focus, and relaxation. They are not therapy, treatment, or a medical intervention. If any exercise causes discomfort, dizziness, panic, pain, or shortness of breath, stop immediately. You are responsible for using breathing exercises in a manner appropriate to your health condition.

Sleep and Mood

Sleep logs, mood check-ins, and related insights are self-management tools only. They are not designed to diagnose sleep disorders, mental health conditions, or other medical concerns.
